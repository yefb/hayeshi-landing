# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

Hayeshi S.A.S. ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

## Our Privacy Philosophy

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. We collect only what is necessary to provide our services and never sell your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Website Visitors

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics of any kind. We do not collect personal information from visitors unless you contact us directly.

### App Users

For information about data collection in our apps, please refer to the specific privacy policy for each app:

- **GardenOS:** <https://gardenos.app/privacy>

## How We Use Information

When you contact us via email, we use your information solely to respond to your inquiry and provide support. We do not add you to marketing lists without explicit consent or share your information with third parties.

## Data Storage

Any information you provide is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to any personal information we hold about you
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of any communications

## Jurisdiction

Hayeshi S.A.S. operates from Colombia and complies with applicable privacy laws.

## Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

**Email:** support@hayeshi.com

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
